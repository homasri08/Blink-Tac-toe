Blink Tac Toe 🧠

🎮 Choose Your Emoji Categories

🧠 How to Play Blink Tac Toe

  • This is a 2-player game played on a 3x3 grid using fun emojis 🎉.
  • Each player chooses an emoji category (Tech, Vehicles, Faces).
  • Players take turns placing a random emoji from their category on the grid.
  • Only 3 emojis per player can be on the board at a time.
  • Placing a 4th emoji will make the oldest one vanish (FIFO rule).
  • You cannot place a new emoji where the oldest one was.
  • First to get 3 emojis in a line (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins! 🏆

Good luck and have fun! 🚀
